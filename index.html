Transforming Spaces Through Interactive Art

My work brings life to transitional spaces where people gather, wait, and move through their day. Using generative algorithms and real-time computation, I create installations that respond to time, light, and sometimes viewer interaction.

Each piece is custom-designed for its environment, considering the architecture, lighting conditions, audience, and brand identity of the space. The result is art that feels both timeless and ever-changing.

Types of Installations & Services

What I Create

Large-Scale Digital Installations

Custom generative artworks for lobby walls, atriums, and prominent architectural features. Typically 8-20 feet wide, these pieces create commanding focal points that evolve continuously throughout the day.

Waiting Room & Corridor Art

Calming, meditative pieces designed specifically for healthcare environments and hospitality spaces. Color palettes and movement patterns can be tailored to promote relaxation and positive emotional responses.

Multi-Screen Arrays

Coordinated installations across multiple displays that work as a cohesive composition. Perfect for long corridors, elevator banks, or conference room entryways.

Brand-Integrated Installations

Artwork that incorporates corporate colors, logo elements, or brand language in subtle, sophisticated ways. Creates visual identity without feeling like signage.

Interactive Pieces

Installations that respond to viewer presence, touch, sound, or time of day. These create engaging experiences that invite participation and memorable moments.

Full-Service Implementation

From initial consultation and concept development through installation, calibration, and ongoing support. I manage all technical aspects including hardware specification, mounting systems, and software deployment.

Live Installation Demonstrations

Interactive Concept Renderings

Below are three live, generative demonstrations running in real-time. Each showcases a different aesthetic approach suited to specific environments.

Corporate Lobby Installation
Color Field Meditation

Flowing vertical color bands shift and blend in real-time, creating a calming, ever-changing composition. Perfect for high-traffic areas where visual serenity is valued. Each band oscillates through complementary hues with soft gradients and subtle movement.

Healthcare Waiting Area
Kinetic Language

Positive, healing words rotate and float through space, creating a meditative focal point. The piece uses gentle motion and calming language to create a sense of peace and hope. Words like "BREATHE," "HEAL," and "CALM" move with 3D perspective and varying opacity.

Luxury Hotel Lobby
Translucent Layers

Overlapping geometric forms with gradient fills create depth through translucency and subtle movement. The composition shifts slowly, revealing new color relationships as layers intersect. Perfect for corporate lobbies and modern office environments seeking sophisticated visual presence.

Technical Approach & Collaboration

Implementation

  • Display Technology High-resolution LED displays, projection systems, or custom-built display arrays depending on space requirements and budget
  • Software Custom generative algorithms built with p5.js, Processing, and WebGL for real-time rendering. All code optimized for 24/7 operation
  • Installation Turnkey installation including hardware specification, software development, mounting systems, and on-site calibration
  • Customization Each piece can be tailored to match brand colors, architectural features, and specific spatial requirements. Optional: integration with building systems, time-of-day variations, seasonal themes
  • Maintenance Remote monitoring and updates available. Installations designed for reliability with redundant systems and easy serviceability
